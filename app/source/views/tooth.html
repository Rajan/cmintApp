Structure of Tooth Swipe
Enamel
White layer of tissue on top of human teeth



Dentin
  • Subtsance between enamel or cementum and the pulp chamber
  • Secreted by the odontoblasts of the dental pulp
  • Mineralized connective tissue with an organic matrix of collagenous proteins
  • Made up of 70% inorganic materials,20% organic materials and 10% water



Pulp
The dental pulp is the central part of the tooth filled with soft connective tissue


Cementum
The layer of bone-like tissue covering the root.It is not as hard as enamel


Gums(Gingiva)
Soft tissue that immediately surrounds the teeth and bone.It protects the bone and the root of the teeth and provides an easily lubricated surface
